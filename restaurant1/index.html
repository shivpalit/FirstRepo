Restaurant Description

The Restaurant was founded in 2010 by Chef Jane Doe and her husband, John. Chef Doe attended the Culinary Institute of America and specialized in traditional South-East Asian cuisines. She has spent years perfecting unique dishes by her culinary studies and research with her native southern American cuisine. The Restaurant is the first of five restaurants founded by Chef Doe, but still remains the only location that serves all of her original fusion recipes. We welcome all to experience Chef Doe's niche culinary inventions and discover a new way to dine.
